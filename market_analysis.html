Summary

First and foremost, the data is biased in terms of company_location (US takes up 81.13% of data)

  1. Machine Learning Scientist, Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer- The highest paid job titles

  2. People with Senior-level experience are the most sought after exp by companies

  3. Qatar pays the highest avg salary. Bosnia and Herzegovina, Qatar, Israel and the United States ranked first in Entry-, Mid-, Senior- and Executive-level seniorities, respectively.

  4. There is an increaing trend of salaries in all exp level from year-to-year.

  5. There is no as-such difference between the salary given for those who work on-site versus the employees working remotely and people who work on a hybrid setup are paid less compared to the other two models.

  6. The avg salary offered by medium-sized companies is almost 30k more than what is offered by large companies.
